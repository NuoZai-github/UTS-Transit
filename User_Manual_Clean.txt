UTS Transit - User Manual

Version: 1.1
Date: December 2025
Team: UTS Transit Development Team

1. INTRODUCTION

UTS Transit is a smart campus mobility solution designated to make transportation at University of Technology Sarawak (UTS) more predictable and efficient. This manual provides instructions for Students, Drivers, and Administrators.

2. GETTING STARTED

System Requirements:
- Mobile App: Android 8.0 or newer / iOS 14 or newer.
- Web Admin Portal: Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox browsers.
- Internet: Active internet connection required.

Installation:
- Android: Install the provided UTSTransit.apk file. (Note: Allow "Install from Unknown Sources" if prompted).
- Web Portal: Access via the provided URL.

3. REGISTRATION GUIDE (IMPORTANT)

Student Registration:
1. Open the App and tap "Sign up here".
2. Enter your Email, Password (min 6 characters), Full Name, and Student ID.
3. Keep the Role selected as "Student".
4. Tap Register.

Driver & Admin Registration (Restricted):
To ensure security, Driver and Admin accounts are restricted.
1. On the Sign Up page, enter your personal details.
2. Select Role as "Driver" or "Admin".
3. You will be required to enter a System Code.
4. Enter the Code: LIME6199
5. Tap Register. (Note: Incorrect codes will prevent registration).

4. STUDENT FEATURES

Booking Trips:
1. On the Home Screen, view the list of available bus schedules.
2. Tap the "Book Trip" button next to your desired time.
3. The button will change to "Booked", confirming your reservation.

Live Bus Tracking:
1. Navigate to the "Map" tab at the bottom.
2. When prompted, select "Allow" or "While using the app" for Location Permissions.
3. You will see a map with your location (Blue Dot) and the Bus (Bus Icon).
4. Observe the Status Bar at the bottom of the screen to see the bus's progress between Hostel and Campus.

5. DRIVER FEATURES

Starting a Journey:
1. Log in with your Driver credentials.
2. On the Console page, select the current trip from the dropdown menu.
3. Check the passenger count.
4. Tap the green "Start Journey" button.
5. Important: Ensure you grant Location Permissions when asked.
6. Your status will update to "Driving" and your location will be broadcast to students.

Ending a Journey:
1. Once you arrive at the destination, tap the red "End Journey" button.
2. Tracking will stop automatically.

6. ADMIN WEB PORTAL

1. Access the Web Admin URL and log in.
2. Dashboard: View overview statistics including total students, drivers, and active buses.
3. Tracking: Click the "Tracking" tab to view a live map of all active buses.
4. Announcements: Use this tab to post system-wide notifications.

7. TROUBLESHOOTING

Issue: Registration Failed
Solution: Ensure your password is at least 6 characters. If registering as Driver, ensure you used the code LIME6199.

Issue: Location Permission Denied
Solution: Go to Phone Settings > Apps > UTSTransit > Permissions > Location, and select "Allow".

Issue: Bus Icon Not Moving
Solution: The driver may not have started the journey yet, or their internet connection is unstable.
